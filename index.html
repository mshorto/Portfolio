Who I am

My skills and experience put me in the unique position of being both a designer and a producer. I spent years as a professional video producer before I turned my attention to UX & UI design. Now I am focused on creating clean, intuitive and user-centered interfaces for web and mobile. I love learning about current design trends and then deciding when to push them. Midnight Express

Midnight Express is a business I created to help alleviate the lack of late-night transportation between San Francisco and Silicon Valley. We use crowdfunding to hire buses that run South along the Caltrain route after 2 a.m.

I did all of the branding, UX and UI design work for the site. I created the logo and built the icons. I designed flyers and social media images for the company. I developed the site using HTML, CSS, JS, JQ and Skeleton.

  • Client: Midnight Express
  • Date: December 2014
  • Service: UX/UI Design & Front-End Development
